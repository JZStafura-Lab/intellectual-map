About

This project maps a personal reading collection to reveal patterns of intellectual inquiry. Rather than organizing by traditional academic disciplines, books are categorized by the fundamental problems they address.

The visualization reveals which domains are well-covered, where gaps exist, and which authors serve as bridges between different modes of thinking. Built with Python, NetworkX, and Plotly.
